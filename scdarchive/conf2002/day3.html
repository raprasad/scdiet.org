Conference on Intestinal Health Through Diet
January 4 - 6, 2002
Tucson, Arizona

Register Now!

If you or a loved one suffers from any of the following:

  • Crohn's disease
  • Ulcerative colitis
  • Irritable Bowel Syndrome
  • Diverticulitis
  • Celiac disease
  • Cystic fibrosis
  • Undiagnosed chronic diarrhea
  • Undiagnosed chronic constipation

Attend this conference to:

  • Understand the impact of diet on intestinal health
  • Learn how to make changes in your diet to reduce symptoms and/or achieve remission
  • Learn shopping tips and cooking techniques to help you heal
  • Meet patients who use the SCD to address IBD, IBS, Celiac disease, and more
  • And much, much more!

Agenda, Day 3:

Best Western Inn Suites, Coronado Room
6201 N. Oracle Rd.
(520) 297-8111

Sunday, January 6th, 2002
9:00 AM - 10:00 AM

  • Workshop: benefits of self-administered acupressure to aid intestinal health. Tucson-based yoga instructor and acupressure expert Lucy Ann McWilliams will conduct a hands-on workshop to teach you about acupressure and show you how to use acupressure to aid healthy digestion.

Please arrive 15-20 minutes early so we can confirm your registration and start on time.

Sunday, January 6th, 2002
10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

  • Discussion: benefits of colon hydrotherapy. Tucson-based colon hydrotherapist Sheila Shea, M.A., L.M.T., B.F.A will host a discussion about the combined benefits of colon hydrotherapy and diet upon intestinal health.

Please arrive 15-20 minutes early so we can confirm your registration and start on time.

Sunday, January 6th, 2002
11:00 AM - 12:00 Noon

  • Conference closing, good-byes.

  • Distribution of printed Conference Proceedings.

Note: Sunday afternoon is intentionally left open so out-of-towners can return home or enjoy some of Tucson's attractions.
