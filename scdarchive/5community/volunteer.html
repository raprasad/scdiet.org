What is SCDiet.org?       


SCDiet.org is an edited, purposeful compilation of communications from a thriving community of thousands of listserve subscribers who are getting real results from dietary adjustments. SCDiet.org serves as an information resource site to IBD and IBS news, patient stories, books, recipes, directories of caregivers, and links to other SCD-related websites and resources. Our target audience includes patients, caregivers, and families coping with diseases, disorders, or chronic illness.

SCDiet.org's goal is to help people with inflammatory bowel disease, celiac disease, irritable bowel syndrome, and other chronic illnesses find their way to sustainable good health through diet. For recent news and updates visit our News page.

SCDiet.org is supported solely by the volunteer efforts of the people listed on the About page, and by occasional donations (in return for advertising space) from SCD Communtiy Supporters.

      

How you can help       

 
Things you can do to help increase awareness of the SCD:

  • Join the SCD listserve and help answer newbie questions
  • Build your own webpage to share your own testimonial / success story, and link it to SCDiet.org
  • Wear our SCD logowear
  • Join the SCD Task Force to get a clinical study to recognize the SCD's benefits, sharing them with the CCFA, doctors, insurance companies, etc.
  • Join the many web forums, newsgroups, and listserves dedicated to IBD, IBS, Celiac, etc. and spread the word to those who have not heard of the SCD
  • Whatever else you can think of!
