This is the SCD web site, run by Mik Aidt as a service to all of us on the SCD, and to those who haven't learned about the SCD yet, and have happened on the site through a link or web search. It has info on the lists, archives (to help new people on the list, especially, who haven't seen the posts), recipes, info on the diet, health problems associated with intestinal problems, sources to buy important SCD ingredients, etc. Perhaps you are a newbie and don't realize that anything you post to any email group is most likely going to be on the list's site, will show up if someone does a net search on your name, etc. New list members are generally not informed that their posts can be in archives because it's assumed that we all know this. This was not a "lapse of courtesy" by Mr. Aidt, who has done a lot of work to help people find the diet and learn how to stay on it.

If this concerns you, and there certainly is an immense problem of a total lack of privacy on the net, you might want to read some of the info on http://www.anonymizer.com/3.0/index.shtml under Resources. If you want your posts to be anonymous, you can use Anonymizer's free anonymous mail feature, you can go to Cyber Cafes and libraries and use different free email addys every time you post to an email list or newsgroup, and you can take other steps to insure that "No one knows you are a dog" when you surf or write emails. You can install Linux, if you haven't already, which is less insecure than Windows, and do all your surfing and emailing with an Anonymizer account, which is very reasonably priced (for a dial-up connection) at $60 per quarter (or you can use your current ISP and surf free through Anonymizer).
