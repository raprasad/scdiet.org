What is the Maelstrom IBD-Diet mailing list?

The IBD-Diet list is a mailing list for group discussion of a variety of therapeutic diets for the treatment of Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD), including those advocated by:
Dr. Wolfgang Lutz in his book, Dismantling the Myth; Dr. Walter Voegtlin's Stone Age Diet; Alun, Jones, et. al. in: Lancet Volume V.II, 1985. Pages 177-180; Elaine Gottschall's Specific Carbohydrate Diet; as described in her book, Breaking the Vicious Cycle; as well as rotating diets and other dietary interventions which are appropriately reported to aid in the health of IBD sufferers.


Purpose of the IBD-Diet Discussion Group

1. To discuss a variety of diets, and the conditions for which they are indicated - Crohn's disease, Ulcerative Colitis, Diverticulitis and Irritable Bowel Syndrome.

2. To exchange ideas, knowledge, tips, and coping skills, and mutual support related to IBD.

3. To encourage comments, discussion, and debate of the merits of various diets.

These are very basic, simple, logical rules. If you would like to add to them, tell us, as this is our support group, not any one person's. We are all here to learn from one another.



Important

In the spirit of information sharing and open exchange, this list is unmoderated and any subscriber can post to it. Therefore you should realize that information obtained from this list might not be accurate or medically appropriate for everyone.
Each reader must draw their own conclusions about how to use any information obtained from this list.
The listowners strongly suggest that each reader do their own analysis of any claims made. In all cases, it is recommended that people consult a medical practitioner first.
