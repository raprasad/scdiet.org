  Books
If you haven't already done so, be sure to purchase a copy of Breaking The Vicious Cycle (BTVC). This book will help you understand the "how's and the why's" about the Specific Carbohydrate Diet, contains lots of Internet resource information, testimonials, recipes, and more.

BTVC is available online at SCDiet.com, Amazon.com, Barnesandnoble.com, Borders.com, LucysKitchenShop.com, and other online bookstores. Your local bookseller may actually carry it (and if not they can order it quite easily from the author and publisher, below).
