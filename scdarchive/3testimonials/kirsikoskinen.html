SCD Testimonials

 
  Kirsi Koskinen's testimonial to the SCD
 

Kirsi is in Finland, and has such tremendous success with the SCD that she now runs the Finnish SCD web site. The following is from a post to the SCD list.

 Visit Kirsi's Finnish SCD web site!

 

Kirsi's Testimonial, in English

Subject: old-timers...news from Finland
Date: Mon, 4 Sep 2000
From: Kirsi Koskinen
To: SCD-list@longisland.com

Shalom dear Elaine!

I am so so sorry, not to write you for so long. I am on the list all the time, but be cause we have our own lively list here in Finland I am not able to follow discussion as closely as I would like to. ...BTW one of our Finnish list told me you missed me so busy I am these days! We got our first SCD-cooking weekend on 11-13. August and it was a success. There were SCDers and our family members all cooking super delicious food and having fun!

I never thought leading such a group and baking could be so cause so much fun. On our egroup-list there is now 23 finnish people and almost all of them have started SCD. I am beginning to make some big Changes with my WEB-page on this week, to get it with additive recipes and info for starters etc.

I cannot imagine how lively our list has become since we started. We got one lady selling almond flour and other dry SCD foods, one father has invented small yoghurt heater, which can be used on car by using tobacco-lightner (?)...we used it on our one and a half week vacation to make SCD-yoghurt and I also ate it on board sailing towards to Stocholm (6th month mark while staying in Sweden)!! ...and one grandpapa making contacts to get BTVC in Finnish. Few days earlier they put my link from CC-foundations web-page to my SCD-WEB-page....every week we are having one or two newbies on board. These things I could tell you over and over again :)

And about my health....First time in 5 years I am full time student in University of Oulu plus having my free-time as I used to have before getting ill. No symphtoms, my blood tests are completely normal and having lots of fun! Bicycling, walking, swimming, travelling, working, e-mailing, studying and enjoying my wonderful new life. The best part is, that I can enjoy seeing when other Finns are getting their life back to track and getting better as I have. Elaine, I have had my all wonderful dreams back.

If I had to choose among every poeple from the whole world one human being, that I would want to see in heaven, it would be you, dear Elaine. I know you have heard this so many times, that it might be hard for you to see it clearly, what a great and blessing work you have done for us and for our families. It is something I wonder every day, when I think about you, Mom.

love
God Bless,
kirsi (Crohn's)

 

And Elaine's reply to Kirsi and the list:

Subject: Re: old-timers...news from Finland
Date: Mon, 4 Sep 2000
From: "Elaine"

To Darling Kirsi from Elaine

Oh, how I enjoyed your letter and I am not cutting and pasting because I want all our new ones to see your inspiring letter. A very fine fellow in Germany is now working on getting a German publisher to buy the rights to BTVC in German and he is translating it into German quickly. Germany is a big country with people who are generally quite receptive to new ideas. I do not speak for the medical profession but come on you medical doctors, we have room on board. Love and best wishes and thanks for your good wishes. We had a blessing with our daughter and from Dr. Haas and every night I pray that God will let his spirit guide me in spreading his word throughout the world. Amen
