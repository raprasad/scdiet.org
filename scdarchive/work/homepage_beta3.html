Welcome to SCDiet.org! The SCD Web Library was founded in 1996 and is an edited, purposeful compilation of listserve communications of thousands of people with Crohn's Disease, Ulcerative Colitis, Celiac Disease, IBD, and IBS who are discussing and getting real results from the Specific Carbohydrate Diet (SCD).

The SCD is a strict grain-free, lactose-free, and sucrose-free dietary regimen, and is detailed in "Breaking The Vicious Cycle", by Elaine Gottschall. This natural approach to intestinal health through diet has been proven in thousands of instances and by countless testimonies to provide relief for those who seek an adjunct therapy to that provided by traditional medicine.

This site is organized into two areas. Please refer to the Beginners' Area for basic information about starting the diet, recipes, frequently asked questions, and for online community and support group listings. Only after reading the information found in Elaine Gottschall's book and the Beginners' area should one venture into the Experienced SCDers Area. The information provided on this web site is in no way meant to be used in place of medicines prescribed by your doctor.

Best wishes for Happy Healing!
- Mik Aidt & Mike Simons, webmasters, SCDiet.org
