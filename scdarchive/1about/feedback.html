SCDiet.org is an ongoing collaborative project by two webmasters who voluntarily archived the discussion of the many subscribers of the SCD listserves between 1996 and 2001.

With the advent of self-archiving email listserves and ancilliary SCD listserves and email groups, since 2001 this site is updated primarily when new research, testimonials, and SCD resources are submitted. Daily discussion, debate, and current news are best found on one of the many SCD listservers and groups.
