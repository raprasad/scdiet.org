Tuesday, July 11, 2000
Folks,
the new design of www.scdiet.org has been "launched". I use quote marks because we recognize that there is still a bit of work to do which we had not realized, but we felt that there was no need to keep everyone waiting.

You will notice that everything (350+ html files) has been organized into one of eight categories. You should be able to find everything that is "edited, cleaned, and polished" in one of the 7 new categories; everthing "as it was in the old design" is still available in the "Archives" section. Please try to find what you are after in the "new" sections first. :-)

If you have any OLD LINKS to pages and content that used to appear here you will need to update them in your notes, your bookmarks, your instructions to others, etc. We have a Search function which is quite nifty, so use it! :-)

Please be patient while we complete a few "fixes" here and there. We have noticed a few broken links, for example. Nevertheless, please use the Feedback form to email Mike with any suggestions regarding content or links (broken or new ones to add).

Cheers,
Mike and Mik

Milestones

  • July, 2000 - Complete redesign of SCDiet.org is made public. Based upon survey feedback, the site's contents have been re-formatted in a way that is makes it easier to find information and is more visually appealing. Mik, Linda, and Mike sit back and savor an SCD-legal beverage, enjoying the glowing pixels of their labor. But not for long; there's always more to add. Stay tuned!

  • March, 2000 - SCDiet.org accepts its first support from Community Advertisers.
    March, 2000 - SCDiet.org moves to Inform.dk's UNIX servers. Net results: better performance, possiblity of more cgi scripting, increased stability.

  • September, 1999 - The "Long Island" SCD listserve's ISP moved and upgraded their software. Digest mode has been improved. We have made some screen snapshots to help you decide how to subscribe: screen shots. Also, the steps for subscribing have changed. New instructions here.

  • August, 1999 - SCDiet.org domain is purchased by Mik; the entire SCD library is moved to this new web address. Be sure to bookmark the new url: www.scdiet.org!
