Conference on Intestinal Health Through Diet
January 4 - 6, 2002
Tucson, Arizona

Register Now!

If you or a loved one suffers from any of the following:

  • Crohn's disease
  • Ulcerative colitis
  • Irritable Bowel Syndrome
  • Diverticulitis
  • Celiac disease
  • Cystic fibrosis
  • Undiagnosed chronic diarrhea
  • Undiagnosed chronic constipation

Attend this conference to:

  • Understand the impact of diet on intestinal health
  • Learn how to make changes in your diet to reduce symptoms and/or achieve remission
  • Learn shopping tips and cooking techniques to help you heal
  • Meet patients who use the SCD to address IBD, IBS, Celiac disease, and more
  • And much, much more!

Agenda, Day 1:

Windmill Suites at St. Philip's Plaza, Santa Rita room
4250 N. Campbell Ave.
(520) 577-0007

Friday, January 4th, 2002
7:00 PM - 9:00 PM

  • Conference kick-off

  • Keynote speech by intestinal health expert Sheila Shea, M.A., L.M.T., B.F.A. Ms. Shea's presentation, "Intestinal Health Through Diet", will explore the nutritional and digestive challenges faced by people with IBD, IBS, and other intestinal disorders, review research on complex carbohydrates, and describe how following the Specific Carbohydrate Diet (SCD) helps people regain their lives.

Please arrive 15-20 minutes early so we can confirm your registration and start on time.
