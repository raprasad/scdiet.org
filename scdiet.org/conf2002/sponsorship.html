The 2002 Conference on Intestinal Health Through Diet is not a commercial event. Our intent is to share our personal experiences and increase awareness of diet as a means to intestinal health. Nevertheless, we are open to sponsorship by those that wish to support people with SCD-related goods and services.

How do I sponsor the Conference on Intestinal Health Through Diet?

First, you must have something to offer our community. We are primarily interested in products or services that are related to intestinal health through diet, and especially the SCD. If you don't know what the SCD is yet, click here.

How much does sponsorship cost?

If you are a company or a commercial venture, we'll ask you to pay a nominal fee of $100. Email us, telling us who you are and why you want to help support this event.

What are the logo display specs?

Your logo display should be a GIF or JPG file that is 140 pixels wide by 70 pixels high. We will also need the URL the graphic will link to. You will need to create your own graphic (but we can probably help you for a nominal fee).

What are the benefits?

There are many benefits to sponsoring the Conference on Intestinal Health Through Diet: Exposure, Exposure, Exposure!

The Conference on Intestinal Health Through Diet is the definitive event for people wishing to learn how to achieve relief from irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), inflammatory bowel disease (IBD; Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis), Celiac disease, candidiasis, and more. The organizers and panelists are totally committed to helping as many people as possible get healthy.

With your sponsorship you get:

  • One half-page advertisement in the Conference Proceedings
  • Logo display with link to your web site or email address
  • One copy of the Conference Proceedings

You can be sure that you will get noticed by the just the right demographic: people who are following a regimen to achieve remission in their illness, and lots of them.

We hope this helps in your decision to sponsor this event. If you have any further questions, or want to sign up, please contact us.

Sincerely yours,

Mike Simons and Laura Tanzer, Conference Organizers
