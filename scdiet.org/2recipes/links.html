SCD Recipes

 
  Links to other recipe websites
 


Here you will find a compilation of interesting recipe websites that we have collected along the way. Almost any recipe you find can be adapted to the SCD standards. Most of these links are NOT "SCD safe". You, have to "interpret" and translate most of the recipes, but all it takes is a bit of experimenting.
For example, almond flour instead of other flours, coconut milk instead of regular milk or cream, honey instead of sugar, etc, etc. And there you have it!

Subject: Update of PaleoFood Recipe Site

I just spent some time reorganizing the PaleoFood recipe site and adding more than 100 KB of new recipe text. This makes it much easier to find things, and the site is now more than 50% larger.

All recipes are gluten-free, casein-free, grain-free, and bean-free. The should make them acceptable for most people on the SCD.

This recipe site is all SCD, plus no dairy at all, and not the few bean types that Elaine allows. It does have citrus and some tomato. Often the recipes with tomatoes are segregated out.

PaleoFood Recipe Collection: http://www.PaleoFood.com/

From an adult point-of-view I like to snack on raw nuts of various types and homemade jerky. But you are looking for more lunch type of things. Check out the site. It is organized like a popular cookbook.
