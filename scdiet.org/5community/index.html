  SCD Events Schedule and Calendar

SCD-ers meet frequently to share recipes, learn, grow, and have a great time! Check the SCD Events page to see if something's happening in your area.

  E-mail

Isn't it nice to hear "You've got mail"? There are several e-mail list servers and online Yahoo! groups to which you can subscribe in order to learn more about the SCD, Crohn's, Colitis, IBS, intestinal health through diet, and more! These email and online group forums are the primary source of online support for SCDers.

  Internet newsgroups

Also known as the Usenet, there are literally thousands of differnent Internet communities where people share knowledge and interests.

The primary newsgroup of interest for Crohn's, Colitis, and IBD is:

alt.support.crohns-colitis

The primary newsgroup of interest for Irritable Bowel Syndrome is:

alt.support.ibs

Contact your Internet Service Provider for more information on how to configure your computer to access Internet newsgroups.
